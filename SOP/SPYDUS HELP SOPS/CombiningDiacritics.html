    List of combining diacritics (non spacing graphic characters)
    In This Topic

    Non spacing graphic characters (diacritics) are always used in conjunction with other spacing graphic characters. More than one non spacing graphic character may be associated with one spacing graphic character. In Unicode, these characters always follow the spacing character they modify. Display of some diacritic characters in the web depends on the ability of the font to display the character.

    See also http://lcweb2.loc.gov/cocoon/codetables/45.html.
