    Renew loans for dependants in Circulation
    In This Topic

    You'll see a link to any dependants in the borrower registration pane. For example: 

    This provides a quick and easy way to renew the loans for all the family members. Here's how.

    1. From the Module Menu select Circulation and then select the head of the family.
    2. Click the Dependants link.
    3. Do one of the following.
      • If there's only one dependant then click the link to their loans.
      • If there's more than one dependant then click Translate Set, Current loans linked to these borrowers and then Translate Set.
    4. Do one of the following.
      • Select the loans you want to renew and then click Renew Selections.
      • Click Renew All.
