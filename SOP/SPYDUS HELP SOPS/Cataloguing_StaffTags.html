    Staff Tags
    In This Topic

    Spydus provides the ability to add local tags or subjects to bibliographic records. These tags are able to be added at a local institution level, or across a consortium network.

    Tags may be added to or removed from bibliographic records, either:

    • on individual bibliographic records (with the Manage Staff Tags option at the record Full Display), 
    • or, to sets of records using the Bulk Update Staff Tags utility
