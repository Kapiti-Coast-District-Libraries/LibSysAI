    Do not display pickup locations from other institutions
    In This Topic

    To specify whether pickup locations from other institutions are available for selection when you place or edit a reservation:

    1. From the Module Menu select Maintenance, Circulation and then General Parameters.
    2. Set Do not display pickup locations from other institutions and Do not display pickup locations from other institutions (OPAC) to Yes or No.
      No Pickup locations from other institutions are displayed.
      Yes pickup locations from other institutions are not displayed.
    3. Click Save or Save & Close.
    Null means the same as No.  

    Pickup locations from another institution will only be displayed if the borrower account already exists in that institution or if the borrower account can be added automatically.        

    If the reservation you're editing already has a pickup location from another institution then we'll display the pickup locations from that institution even if Do not display... is set to Yes. 

    These parameters are not used in global OPAC.
