    Navigate from an article to its parent
    In This Topic

    If you’re in one of the article filters and you navigate to the parent bibliographic record or issue record then the filter will be removed. This means that you’ll see all available records, i.e. all records in the active filters. This is the same as when you’re viewing records in a SavedList — no collection filters are applied so you can view all available records.

    Similarly if you save your article record and then view the SavedList, you’ll see that the authorities will be hyperlinked when you navigate to the parent.

    There are three filters that can be in action at any time — the collection filter, access filter and institution filter.
